About Us

Code2Gather is a platform where every coding aspirant can interact with other programmers and together can make a great team that helps each other and develop their own skill by getting and providing support to other community members. What makes us different from other platforms is that:

  • You can make programs simultaneously while discussing with community members
  • Receive latest news and technologies and try them by not going to any other platform
  • You can have a contact of the community member if you are interested in making projects with him/her.

Code2Gather brings a great support to young and aspiring coding aspirants as this platform is like a big mall in which everything they need to accomplish their work will be provided immediately.
